# ITAI 4370 Course Portfolio Rubric Checklist

## Completeness of required artifacts (5%)
- Selected works include diagrams, laboratory graphs, code, and the Final Exam Portfolio.
- Project documentation covers problem statements, methods, tools, results, and interpretations.
- Assessment artifacts list Assignments 01–04, Laboratories 01–05, the midterm, quiz, and capstone.

## Quality of reflections (4%)
- Reflection 1 explains how telecommunications understanding evolved.
- Reflection 2 explains how AI was applied to telecommunications problems.
- Reflection 3 explains improved skills and future growth.

## Clarity and professionalism (3%)
- Responsive navigation, descriptive headings, accessible image text, and consistent design.
- Downloadable evidence is grouped with the project it supports.

## Evidence of progression (3%)
- Timeline moves from signals and topologies to 5G, Open RAN, AI optimization, edge AI, security, sustainability, digital twins, 6G, and ethics.
